Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any more questions, send us an email at info@kinet-x.org

How much does Kinet-X cost?

The estimated tuition for our Fall 2016 program is $975, including activities, & material for the duration of the program. We don't want price to prevent you from attending Kinet-X, so need-based financial aid is available!


Who teaches the classes?

Classes are taught by Kinet-X staff, people with background in STEM and the engineering field.


What grade levels do you accept?

We accept students currently attending the 5th to 9th grades.


When and where is the program?

The Fall 2016 program will begin on October 1 and run through January 7, with days off on holidays. Sessions will be held from 10AM - 5PM on Saturdays. It will be hosted at the Pine Street School near Wall Street.


Do you need prior experience for this program?

No. We look for candidates who show initiative, action-orientation, and have a good fit with our values. These students show to us they have what it takes to be successful innovators. There are no minimum grade or activity involvement requirements.


What makes Kinet-X unique?

We view ourselves less as an enrichment or academic program, and more as an incubator, designed to inspire and train teens to create projects with practical use. We focus on real-world applicability throughout the program; we minimize classroom time and highly value learning by doing. We prepare students for future careers like no other program.


When is our application deadline?

Our early application deadline is August 31, 2016, and our regular application deadline is September 14, 2016. We recommend you apply as early as possible!
