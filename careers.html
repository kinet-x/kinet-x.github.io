Public Relations Intern

Kinet-X is seeking an unpaid public relations intern to take our community involvement and local media relations to the next level. Under the direction of our Operations Director and Marketing Director, you will work with local schools and local newspapers to increase exposure and grow community involvement.

The ideal candidate is a hustling self-starter who flexes excellent communication and organizational skills. Kinet-X is a fast paced environment that requires rapid responses and a determined team.

Responsibilities

  • Work with the Operations & Marketing Director to maintain contact with principals and parter schools over the year.
  • Write and communicate press releases to local news organizations and other media outlets.
  • Maintain good relationships with community organizations that provide support to Kinet-X.

The Ideal Candidate

  • Attending high school in the Fall of 2016
  • Ability to communicate in corporate environments.
  • Ability to work well with teammates.

Apply

Complete the application below and email your resume to tahseen@kinet-x.org.
A professional resume is not required but preferred.
An summary of experiences you have had and how it would help you with this job would be great!
