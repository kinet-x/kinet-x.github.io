  • Our Mission

    To inspire and prepare the new generation for the challenges ahead of them. In the past 60 years, technology has advanced at an extremely rapid pace- and along with it come more and more problems that need to be solved. Kinet-X aims to make sure everyone has access to STEM, no matter who they are or where they're from.

We think inventors are most productive when they can spend most of their time inventing. Our goal at Kinet-X is to create an environment where students can exclusively learn, focus, and build something awesome. Accepted students will launch a 4-week process to develop a real, viable, practical invention. The estimated cost of our summer 2016 program is $650, but need-based financial aid is available.
