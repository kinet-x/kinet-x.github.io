Accelerating Innovation

Kinet-X is an intensive and effective program for middle/high schoolers that gets them building and inventing.
It’s focused on both engineering and programming, giving teens the thorough introduction into
STEM that's currently missing in today’s education landscape.

Weeks 1-3

Introduction
Begin with core concepts. You'll learn the basics of engineering and programming, including the parts of a circuit, structure of a web page, and basic HTML/CSS.



Weeks 4-6

Skill Development
Using the skills acquired from Weeks 1-3, you will begin to create your own projects and expand your knowledge base through practice; creating things like sound-to-light sensors, LED circuits and website skeletons.



Weeks 7-9

Advanced
Now that the basics have been learned and applied, it's time to apply that knowledge and create great things. You will learn how to use CSS/JS to structure and bring life to your web pages, as well as be introduced to advanced parts- such as motors- to create things like a solar powered car.



Weeks 10-12

Creation
Weeks of knowledge will cumulate into your own final project- you decide what you create! Options range from creating your own electrical creation- and creating a web marketing platform- to beefing up your solar powered car, to creating your a web application and marketing it. You choose what you want to be proud of!
