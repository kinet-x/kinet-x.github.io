Accelerating Innovation

Kinet-X is an intensive and affordable program for middle/high schoolers that gets students building and inventing.
It’s focused on engineering and programming, giving teens an extensive introduction into
STEM currently missing in today’s education landscape.

  • Weeks 1-3

    Introduction
    Start off the program learning the core concepts of programming and engineering. You will learn engineering concepts like control flow and with that you will build a basic circuit.

  • Weeks 4-6

    Skill Development
    Next, you will take the basic skills you've learned and supplement those with more advanced concepts, such as building sound-to-light circuits and cipher/encryption programs.

  • Weeks 7-9

    Fusion
    Now, you take the engineering and programming topics you learned over the past few weeks and combine them. You'll make a solar-powered car and your very first website!

  • Weeks 10-12

    Creation
    The best part of the course; you can use the skills you learned to define, design, and develop your very own project. Former projects include a personal website and a remote-controlled metal detector car.

The Program

Students are taught in-person at our state of the art location in Downtown Manhattan. In twelve weeks, you can expect to learn all the basic skills necessary to step into the world of STEM. At Kinet-X, you can expect a learning experience that is project-based, combining comprehensive education and unmatched affordability.
