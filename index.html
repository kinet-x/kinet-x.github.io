Accelerating Innovation

Kinet-X is an intensive and affordable program for middle/high schoolers that gets students building and inventing.
It’s focused on both engineering and programming, giving teens the extensive introduction into
STEM that's currently missing in today’s education landscape.

Weeks 1-3

Introduction
Begin with core concepts. You'll learn the basics of engineering and programming, including the parts of a circuit, structure of a web page, and basic HTML tags.



Weeks 4-6

Skill Development
Using the skills acquired from week 1, you will begin to create your own projects and expand your knowledge base through practice; creating things like sound-to-light sensors, LED circuits and website skeletons.



Weeks 7-9

Advanced
Now that the basics have been learned and applied, it's time to apply that knowledge and make really great things. You will learn how to use CSS to structure and bring life to your web pages, as well as be introduced to advanced parts- such as motors- to create things like your own solar powered car.



Weeks 10-12

Creation
Weeks of knowledge will cumulate into your own final project- you decide what you create! Options range from creating your own electrical creation- and making a website to market it- to beefing up your solar powered car to creating your own personal website and uploading it to the web. You choose what you want to be proud of!

The Program

Students are taught in-person at our state of the art location in Downtown Manhattan. In twelve weeks, you can expect to learn all the skills necessary to step into the world of STEM. At Kinet-X, you can expect a learning experience that is project-based, combining comprehensive education and unmatched affordability.
