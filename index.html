The Problem

Many young students begin with an interest in technology, but that interest fades away due to a lack of worthwhile opportunities. Many schools don't provide substantial STEM education, and outside programs are either too expensive or unsubstantial. Kinet-X works with the community to provide an affordable, comprehensive, project-based learning experience. We teach students to become innovators - the Kinet-X way.

Accelerating Innovation

Kinet-X is an intensive program for middle/high schoolers that gets you building and inventing.
It’s focused on engineering and programming, giving teens a comprehensive introduction
into STEM currently missing in today’s education landscape.

  • Weeks 1-3

    Introduction
    Learn the core concepts of Programming & Engineering.

  • Weeks 4-6

    Skill Development
    Begin to acquire advanced concepts/skills

  • Weeks 7-9

    Sensor Application
    Learn how to combine engineering and programming

  • Weeks 10-12

    Creation
    Define, design, and develop your own project.
